Our Story

Founded in 2019 by two friends who loved tea and conversation, Steep & Sage started as a tiny stall at a weekend market. Today we are a neighborhood tea room focused on sourcing small-batch leaves, celebrating seasonal tisanes, and creating slow spaces for connection.

Mission

Foster calm, connection, and care through thoughtfully brewed tea.

Approach

Direct-sourced teas, seasonal menus, workshops, and composting used leaves.

Values

Quality, Community, Sustainability, and Mindfulness.

Sourcing & Sustainability

We partner with small tea farms practicing regenerative agriculture. Leaves are sourced transparently and packaging is recyclable. Spent leaves are composted for community gardens.

Events

We host matcha workshops, tea tastings, and occasional music nights. Sign up via our newsletter or contact us to host a private tasting.
